Host-Guest Agreement

OnPurpose Platform - Connection, Not Dating

Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Platform Mission

OnPurpose facilitates meaningful connections through structured hospitality experiences. This is NOT a dating platform.

1. Host Commitments

As an OnPurpose host, you agree to:

  • Professional Conduct: Maintain appropriate, non-romantic interactions
  • Service Quality: Deliver experiences as described in your profile
  • Punctuality: Arrive on time and honor confirmed bookings
  • Safety: Meet in appropriate public or professional locations
  • Communication: Respond promptly to guest inquiries
  • Expertise: Share genuine knowledge and skills in your category

2. Guest Commitments

As an OnPurpose guest, you agree to:

  • Respectful Behavior: Treat hosts professionally and courteously
  • Clear Expectations: Communicate your learning goals and interests
  • Punctuality: Arrive on time for scheduled experiences
  • Payment: Complete payment as agreed through the platform
  • Feedback: Provide honest, constructive reviews
  • Boundaries: Respect host guidelines and personal boundaries

3. Experience Categories

OnPurpose offers structured experiences in five categories:

  • Local Expert: Neighborhood tours, hidden gems, local insights
  • Cultural Guide: Art, history, museums, cultural experiences
  • Wellness Coach: Fitness, meditation, health, mindfulness
  • Creative Mentor: Art, music, writing, design guidance
  • Professional Networker: Industry insights, career advice, networking

4. Safety Guidelines

  • Public Meetings: Initial meetings should occur in public spaces
  • Identity Verification: Both parties must complete platform verification
  • Emergency Contacts: Share meeting details with trusted contacts
  • Reporting: Report any safety concerns immediately to OnPurpose
  • Trust Your Instincts: Cancel if you feel uncomfortable

5. Prohibited Activities

The following are strictly forbidden on OnPurpose:

  • Romantic advances or dating-related activities
  • Sexual harassment or inappropriate behavior
  • Discrimination based on any protected characteristics
  • Illegal activities or services
  • Circumventing platform payment or communication systems
  • Sharing personal contact information before meeting

6. Payment and Cancellation

  • Platform Fee: OnPurpose charges 20% commission on all bookings
  • Payment Security: All transactions processed through Stripe Connect
  • Cancellation Policy: 24-hour notice required for cancellations
  • Refunds: Available for justified cancellations per platform policy
  • Host Earnings: Released 2-3 business days after experience completion

7. Communication Standards

  • All initial communication must occur through the OnPurpose platform
  • Messages should be professional and experience-focused
  • Personal contact information should only be shared after booking confirmation
  • Platform reserves the right to monitor communications for safety

8. Quality Assurance

  • Host Approval: All hosts undergo verification and approval process
  • Reviews: Both parties can leave reviews after experiences
  • Quality Standards: Consistently poor reviews may result in account suspension
  • Continuous Improvement: OnPurpose monitors and improves experience quality

9. Dispute Resolution

In case of disputes:

  • Contact OnPurpose support immediately
  • Provide detailed information about the issue
  • Participate in mediation process if required
  • Accept platform decisions on refunds and account actions

10. Agreement Acceptance

By using OnPurpose, both hosts and guests acknowledge they have read, understood, and agree to abide by this Host-Guest Agreement and all platform Terms of Service.
